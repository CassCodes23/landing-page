About Us

The Cozy Canine Salon & Suites is a full service pet facility providing quality pet care. We are guided by strong ethics, honesty and professionalism. Our goal is to provide safe and secure accommodations, while promoting a friendly and caring environment for all visiting pets.

The Cozy Canine Salon & Suites is a 9,000 square foot facility that opened in March 2016. We have an in-ground swimming pool that was built specifically for the dogs. It slopes from 6 to 30 inches at it’s deepest end, the perfect size for all dogs. We have a very large Doggie Day Care room filled with toys and lots of love, as well as webcams so you can watch all day! After the first visit, the dogs are pulling their owners across the lobby to get back to day care because they love it so much!

We have 10 Penthouse Suites, these are private rooms that were built to spoil your pet in. We have 17 Presidential Suites that are oversized and 9 of which have doggie doors for access to their own fenced in patio. We also have 42 economy quarters and another 18 economy indoor/outdoor runs.

Five others have low to the floor pool view windows. All of our suites have 19″ color televisions, and web cams so you can log into our website (from your laptop or personal computer) and see exactly what your pet is doing. If your pet is scheduled for day care or pool play time while they are boarding with us, you can watch them from the live doggie cams that are open to the public every day.

We offer a variety of services to meet your pet needs!

Here at The Cozy Canine Salon & Suites, we offer quality pet care and grooming services. Please navigate through all of our available services and options to find what will work best for you.

Bath and Brush -

Upon arrival, you'll consult with a Master Groomer to discuss your pet's needs and determine the best services. If needed, we offer specialty services such as de-matting or de-shedding for an additional fee. Our groomers will always inform you if these services are necessary.

Our Standard Grooming Package Includes:

  • Sudsy Bath

  • Ear Cleaning & Sanitizing
  • Nail Trim
  • Anal Gland Expression

Extra Care: We provide extra attention and care for senior dogs, puppies, and dogs with special needs or health concerns.

Doggy Daycare -

Is your dog spending the day at home sad and alone? Bring your pet to Professional Pet Care for a day of love and stimulation. We offer a premier place for you to leave small and medium sized dogs in a worry-free, safe environment. Our cycle-based playtimes ensure our fur-members have time to rest in their rooms between their action-packed adventures to avoid overheating, exhaustion, and bloat.

We’ve also installed medical-grade HVAC systems that produce 100% fresh, outside filtered air at all times into the facility. With over 10 years of experience in pet care, your pups will be in amazing hands and have the best day ever

Pet Boarding -

Here at Professional Pet Care we offer a home environment in our friendly building, thus making a seamless stress-free transition for your favorite 4 legged friend from your home to ours. There are no crates and only allow a handful of guests at any one time. There is plenty of room to hang out on our sofa. We offer free pick-up/drop-off within the 5 miles, based upon availability. Best yet, we are only steps away from the park, open green fields, and dog runs. Hope to hear from you soon.
